<h4>Vision</h4>
<p>We aim to build strong, sustainable relationships with our alumni through
active engagement. The school recognises that our alumni are made up of
diverse individuals from different age groups who have achieved success
in various fields, and are willing to give back to their alma mater.</p>
<h4>Our Philosophy to Alumni Engagement</h4>
<p>Our alumni hold a cherished place in our school community. They enrich
the spirit of the school, nurture a deep sense of pride, and help keep
cherished traditions alive. As links between the past and present, they
play a meaningful role in shaping the school’s journey forward, walking
alongside teachers in realising our shared vision and mission. Through
their stories and life experiences, alumni offer inspiration that can benefit
our current students.</p>

<p><strong>Alumni Connect Event</strong>
</p>
<p>Every year, we organise a reunion event for our alumni and last year,
we saw ex-Navalites from 1981 to 2023 come back to school for an epic day
of sports – badminton, basketball and football. They also explored familiar
and new learning spaces like The Horizon (library), Legacy (heritage gallery)
and Whitebox (art gallery). We were especially glad to welcome more alumni
who joined us this year, making the event even more memorable! Alumni Connect,
now in its second year, continues to bring our Navalites together, serving
up friendship, nostalgia, and a delicious breakfast! Here’s to many more
moments like these!</p>
