<p>The NBSS PE Department believes that our curriculum should not only promote
students' physical, but also mental and social well-being. Furthermore,
each student should be empowered to achieve success, regardless of physical
attributes.&nbsp;</p>
<p>Sports and physical activities create both planned and spontaneous teachable
moments through their emotional and physical demands. The disciplined preparation
phase - from training to maintaining proper nutrition - coupled with the
unpredictable nature of actual performance, naturally develops character
and essential life skills in our students.</p>
<p>Building on this foundation, our Physical Education (PE) Department leads
the school's Learning for Life Programme (LLP), shaping students holistically
through sports. Our comprehensive PE programme develops broad-based physical
competencies while creating meaningful recreational opportunities that
nurture students' mental and emotional growth.</p>
<p><strong>Programme Reach</strong>
</p>
<p>Our PE programme engages every student in the school, ensuring that all
students, regardless of their athletic ability, have opportunities to develop
physically, mentally, and emotionally through sports and physical activities.</p>
<p><strong>Our Goals</strong>
</p>
<p>Aligned with MOE Physical Education Teaching &amp; Learning Syllabus 2024,
we aim to develop students who demonstrate:</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Movement Competence: Confidence and competence
in various physical and outdoor activities</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Healthy Lifestyle Practices: Personal commitment
to physical activity, nutrition, sleep, outdoor time and hygiene</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Safety Mindset: Ability to assess and manage
risks in daily activities, considering self, others and environment</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Core Values: Sound values-based judgement in
personal behaviour and social interactions</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Enjoyment: Lasting appreciation for physical
activities and healthy living</p>
<p><strong>Curriculum</strong>
</p>
<p>Our structured programme caters to different levels:</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Secondary 1: Badminton and Basketball</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Secondary 2: Softball and Frisbee</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Secondary 3: Volleyball and Floorball</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Secondary 4/5: Sports Elective Modules</p>
<p><strong>Signature Events</strong>
</p>

<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Road Run</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Sports Carnival</p>
<p>●&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Fitness Challenge (during Health and Wellness
