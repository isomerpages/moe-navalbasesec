<h4>Overview</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/DA104815__1_.jpg">
</div>
<p>The NBSS Table Tennis Team is a dynamic and competitive sports group that
proudly represents the school in various competitions. With a strong emphasis
on skill development, teamwork, and sportsmanship, the team strives to
bring out the very best in every student.</p>
<p>The team follows a rigorous training programme that focuses on:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p><strong>Technical skills</strong>: Enhancing stroke play, spin control,
footwork, and game strategy.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Physical conditioning</strong>: Improving agility, reflexes, and
endurance for peak performance.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Tactical awareness</strong>: Developing match strategies and the
ability to adapt to different playing styles.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Mental strength</strong>: Building resilience, focus, and confidence
in high-pressure situations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4>What We Do</h4>
<p><strong>Training Sessions:</strong> Every Monday and Thursday, 3:00pm to
5:30pm</p>
<p><strong>Competitions:</strong> National School Games, OTH Team Challenge
Competition, Friendly Games with Other Schools</p>
