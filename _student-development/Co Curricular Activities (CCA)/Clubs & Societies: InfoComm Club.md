<h4>Overview</h4>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Infocomm_School_Website_Photo.png">
</div>
<p>The Infocomm Club offers students the opportunity to explore the world
of digital media and coding. Through hands-on activities, members develop
skills in photography, video editing, and programming while working on
real-world projects. They also take on key roles in school events, such
as media coverage and tech support. Beyond technical skills, the club fosters
creativity, collaboration, and problem-solving, preparing students for
the digital future.</p>
<h4>What We Do</h4>

</p>

<p>Sec 2 – Tues and Thurs 3 – 5.15 pm,</p>
<p>Sec 3 – Tues 3- 5.15 pm</p>
<p><strong>Competitions/Performances:</strong> Our School Our Stories, National
Youth Tech Championship, National Robotics Competition (2025 onwards),
etc</p>
<p><strong>Workshops &amp; Learning Opportunities:</strong> DSLR Photography
workshop, Film making workshops, Arduino Coding workshops, Student Initiated
VIA projects (media related), Learning journeys to local media companies</p>
<p><strong>Camps &amp; Bonding Activities:</strong> Termly team bonding activities
conducted by Exco members</p>
